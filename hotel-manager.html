Hotel Manager

Why?

Hotel management is a dynamic career that involves overseeing the operations of hotels and ensuring excellent guest experiences.

Opportunities

Hotel managers can work in luxury hotels, resorts, or boutique hotels. Opportunities also exist in event management and hospitality consulting.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong leadership, communication, and organizational skills are essential. A degree in hotel management or related fields is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IHM, EHL, and Cornell University.

Exams

Entrance exams like NCHM JEE and university-specific tests are common for hotel management programs.

Additional Information

Hotel managers need to stay updated with hospitality trends and continuously enhance their skills through training and certifications.
