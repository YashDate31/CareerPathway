Pharmacist

Why?

Pharmacy is a vital career that involves preparing and dispensing medications, ensuring patient safety, and promoting health.

Opportunities

Pharmacists can work in hospitals, retail pharmacies, or as researchers. Opportunities also exist in pharmaceutical marketing and regulatory affairs.

Strengths/Preparation

Attention to detail, medical knowledge, and communication skills are essential. A degree in pharmacy is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include Jamia Hamdard, NIPER, and University of California, San Francisco.

Exams

Relevant exams include GPAT and NAPLEX (for international practice).

Additional Information

Specializations in clinical pharmacy or pharmacovigilance can open advanced career paths.
