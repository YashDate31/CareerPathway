Singer

Why?

Singing is a creative career that allows individuals to express emotions and connect with audiences through music.

Opportunities

Singers can work in the music industry, perform live, or collaborate with media houses. Opportunities also exist in teaching and composing.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong vocal skills, creativity, and stage presence are essential. Training in music and voice modulation is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include Berklee College of Music, Trinity College London, and KM Music Conservatory.

Exams

No specific exams, but certifications in music can enhance credibility.

Additional Information

Building a strong portfolio and networking are key to success in this field.
