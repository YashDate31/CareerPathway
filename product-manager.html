Product Manager

Why?

Product management is a dynamic career that involves overseeing the development and success of products in various industries.

Opportunities

Product managers can work in tech companies, startups, or manufacturing. Opportunities also exist in consulting and marketing.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, leadership, and knowledge of market trends are essential. A degree in business or engineering is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include Stanford, IIMs, and MIT Sloan.

Exams

Relevant exams include GMAT, CAT, and GRE.

Additional Information

Certifications like Scrum Master or Agile can enhance career prospects.
