Firefighter

Why?

Firefighting is a heroic career that involves saving lives, protecting property, and ensuring public safety during emergencies.

Opportunities

Firefighters can work in municipal fire departments, industrial safety teams, or disaster response units. Opportunities also exist in fire safety training and consultancy.

Strengths/Preparation

Physical fitness, quick decision-making, and teamwork are essential. Training at fire academies and certifications in fire safety are required.

Colleges

Institutions like National Fire Service College and state fire training academies offer specialized training.

Exams

Recruitment exams and physical fitness tests are common for entry into fire services.

Additional Information

Firefighters need to stay updated with safety protocols and continuously train to handle diverse emergency situations.
