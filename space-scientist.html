Space Scientist

Why?

Space science is an exciting career that explores the universe and contributes to advancements in space technology.

Opportunities

Space scientists can work in space agencies, research institutions, or academia. Opportunities also exist in satellite technology and astrophysics.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, curiosity, and expertise in physics or engineering are essential. A degree in space science or related fields is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include ISRO, NASA, and Caltech.

Exams

Relevant exams include GATE, GRE, and ISRO entrance tests.

Additional Information

Engaging in research projects and internships in space technology can boost career prospects.
