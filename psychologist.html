Psychologist

Why?

Psychology is a fulfilling career that helps individuals understand and improve mental health and behavior.

Opportunities

Psychologists can work in hospitals, schools, or private practice. Opportunities also exist in research and corporate counseling.

Strengths/Preparation

Empathy, analytical skills, and knowledge of psychological theories are essential. A degree in psychology is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include NIMHANS, University of Cambridge, and Stanford University.

Exams

Relevant exams include GRE (for international studies) and state-level entrance tests.

Additional Information

Specializations in clinical, counseling, or industrial psychology can enhance career prospects.
