Pilot

Why?

Becoming a pilot is a thrilling career that allows individuals to navigate the skies and connect the world.

Opportunities

Pilots can work in commercial airlines, private aviation, or as flight instructors. Opportunities also exist in the military and cargo transport.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong decision-making skills, physical fitness, and technical knowledge are essential. A commercial pilot license (CPL) is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include Indira Gandhi Institute of Aeronautics, CAE Oxford Aviation Academy, and Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University.

Exams

Relevant exams include DGCA exams and FAA tests (for international licenses).

Additional Information

Gaining flight hours and maintaining medical fitness are crucial for career advancement.
