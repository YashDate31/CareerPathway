Supply Chain Manager

Why?

Supply chain management is a critical career that ensures the efficient flow of goods and services in various industries.

Opportunities

Supply chain managers can work in manufacturing, retail, or logistics. Opportunities also exist in consulting and operations management.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong organizational skills, problem-solving, and knowledge of supply chain tools are essential. A degree in logistics or business is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, MIT, and Michigan State University.

Exams

Relevant exams include CAT, GMAT, and certifications like CSCP.

Additional Information

Experience in managing logistics and supply chain operations is crucial for career growth.
