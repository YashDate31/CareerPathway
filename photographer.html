Photographer

Why Choose Photography as a Career?

Photography is a creative profession that captures moments, tells stories, and preserves memories. It is ideal for individuals with an artistic eye and technical skills.

Opportunities

Photographers can work in various fields such as fashion, journalism, wildlife, and event photography. Freelancing and entrepreneurship are also popular options.

Strengths/Preparation

To excel as a photographer, one should develop skills in composition, lighting, and editing. Building a strong portfolio is essential for career growth.
