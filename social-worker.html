Social Worker

Why?

Social work is a noble career that involves helping individuals and communities improve their well-being and quality of life.

Opportunities

Social workers can work in NGOs, government agencies, or healthcare. Opportunities also exist in counseling and community development.

Strengths/Preparation

Empathy, communication skills, and knowledge of social policies are essential. A degree in social work is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include TISS, University of Chicago, and University of California, Berkeley.

Exams

Relevant exams include NET (for teaching roles) and state-level entrance tests.

Additional Information

Fieldwork and internships in community projects can provide valuable experience.
