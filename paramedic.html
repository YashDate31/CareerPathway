Paramedic

Why?

Paramedics play a crucial role in emergency medical services, providing immediate care and saving lives during critical situations.

Opportunities

Paramedics can work in ambulances, hospitals, or as part of disaster response teams. Opportunities also exist in teaching and training.

Strengths/Preparation

Quick decision-making, medical knowledge, and physical fitness are essential. A diploma or degree in paramedical sciences is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include AIIMS, CMC Vellore, and University of Pittsburgh.

Exams

Relevant exams include EMT certification exams and state licensing tests.

Additional Information

Continuous training and certifications in advanced life support can boost career growth.
