Yoga Instructor

Why?

Yoga instruction is a fulfilling career that promotes physical and mental well-being through ancient practices.

Opportunities

Yoga instructors can work in wellness centers, as freelancers, or in corporate wellness programs. Opportunities also exist in teaching and retreats.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong communication skills, physical fitness, and knowledge of yoga techniques are essential. Certification in yoga instruction is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include S-VYASA, Kaivalyadhama, and Yoga Alliance certified schools.

Exams

No specific exams, but certifications in yoga instruction are mandatory.

Additional Information

Continuous practice and staying updated with new techniques are key to success.
