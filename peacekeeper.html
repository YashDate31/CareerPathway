Peacekeeper

Why?

Peacekeeping is a noble career that involves maintaining peace and security in conflict zones, ensuring stability and safety for communities.

Opportunities

Peacekeepers can work with international organizations like the UN, government agencies, or as independent consultants. Opportunities also exist in conflict resolution and humanitarian aid.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong leadership, communication skills, and knowledge of international relations are essential. Training in peacekeeping operations is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include JNU, Harvard Kennedy School, and University of Geneva.

Exams

Relevant exams include Civil Services Examination and UN Peacekeeping Selection Tests.

Additional Information

Experience in conflict zones and multilingual skills are highly valued.
