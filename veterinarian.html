Veterinarian

Why?

Veterinary medicine is a compassionate career that involves caring for animals and ensuring their health and well-being.

Opportunities

Veterinarians can work in clinics, zoos, or as researchers. Opportunities also exist in wildlife conservation and academia.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, empathy, and knowledge of animal anatomy are essential. A degree in veterinary science is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include IVRI, Cornell University, and University of Edinburgh.

Exams

Relevant exams include NEET and GRE (for international studies).

Additional Information

Specializations in areas like surgery or wildlife medicine can enhance career prospects.
