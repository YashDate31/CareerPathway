Investment Banker

Why?

Investment banking is a lucrative career that involves helping organizations raise capital and manage financial transactions.

Opportunities

Investment bankers can work in financial institutions, consulting firms, or as independent advisors. Opportunities also exist in mergers and acquisitions and equity research.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, financial knowledge, and communication skills are essential. A degree in finance or related fields is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, ISB, and Wharton School.

Exams

Entrance exams like CAT, GMAT, and CFA are common for investment banking roles.

Additional Information

Investment bankers need to stay updated with market trends and continuously enhance their skills through certifications and training programs.
