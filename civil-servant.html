Civil Servant

Why?

Becoming a civil servant is a prestigious career that offers the opportunity to serve the nation, implement policies, and contribute to societal development.

Opportunities

Civil servants work in various administrative roles, including IAS, IPS, and IFS. They handle governance, law enforcement, and international relations.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, leadership, and a deep understanding of current affairs are essential. Rigorous preparation for competitive exams is crucial.

Colleges

While no specific college is required, institutions like Delhi University and JNU are popular among aspirants for their resources and guidance.

Exams

The UPSC Civil Services Examination is the primary gateway to becoming a civil servant in India.

Additional Information

Civil servants play a critical role in nation-building. Continuous learning and adaptability are key to excelling in this field.
