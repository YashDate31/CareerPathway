Interior Designer

Why?

Interior designing is a creative career that involves enhancing the functionality and aesthetics of spaces to meet client needs.

Opportunities

Interior designers can work in design firms, architecture companies, or as freelancers. Opportunities also exist in furniture design and space planning.

Strengths/Preparation

Creativity, an eye for detail, and knowledge of design software are essential. A degree in interior design or related fields is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include NID, CEPT University, and Pratt Institute.

Exams

Entrance exams like NID DAT and CEED are common for admission to top design colleges.

Additional Information

Interior designers need to stay updated with design trends and continuously enhance their skills through workshops and certifications.
