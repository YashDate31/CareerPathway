Stock Analyst

Why?

Stock analysis is a lucrative career that involves evaluating financial data to guide investment decisions.

Opportunities

Stock analysts can work in investment firms, banks, or as independent consultants. Opportunities also exist in financial journalism and academia.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, knowledge of financial markets, and attention to detail are essential. A degree in finance or economics is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, London School of Economics, and Wharton School.

Exams

Relevant exams include CFA, FRM, and NISM certifications.

Additional Information

Staying updated with market trends and gaining practical experience through internships can enhance career prospects.
