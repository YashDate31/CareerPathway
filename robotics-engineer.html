Robotics Engineer

Why?

Robotics engineering is an innovative career that involves designing and building robots to solve real-world problems.

Opportunities

Robotics engineers can work in manufacturing, healthcare, or research. Opportunities also exist in AI and automation industries.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong technical skills, creativity, and knowledge of programming are essential. A degree in robotics or mechanical engineering is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include MIT, IITs, and Carnegie Mellon University.

Exams

Relevant exams include GATE and GRE (for international studies).

Additional Information

Hands-on experience with robotics projects and internships can boost career prospects.
