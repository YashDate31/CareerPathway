Logistics Manager

Why?

Logistics management is a critical career that involves planning and coordinating the movement of goods and services efficiently.

Opportunities

Logistics managers can work in supply chain companies, e-commerce, or manufacturing. Opportunities also exist in transportation and warehouse management.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong organizational skills, problem-solving abilities, and knowledge of supply chain management are essential. A degree in logistics or related fields is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, NITIE, and MIT.

Exams

Entrance exams like CAT, GMAT, and university-specific tests are common for logistics management programs.

Additional Information

Logistics managers need to stay updated with industry trends and continuously enhance their skills through certifications and training programs.
