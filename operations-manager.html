Operations Manager

Why?

Operations management is a critical career that involves overseeing business operations to ensure efficiency and productivity.

Opportunities

Operations managers can work in corporate firms, manufacturing, or logistics. Opportunities also exist in project management and consulting.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong leadership, problem-solving skills, and knowledge of business processes are essential. A degree in operations management or related fields is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, Harvard Business School, and INSEAD.

Exams

Relevant exams include CAT, GMAT, and GRE.

Additional Information

Certifications like Six Sigma and PMP can enhance career prospects.
