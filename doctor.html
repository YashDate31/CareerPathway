Doctor

Why?

Becoming a doctor is a noble profession that allows you to save lives, improve health, and make a significant impact on society. It is a career that combines science, compassion, and problem-solving.

Opportunities

Doctors can specialize in various fields such as surgery, pediatrics, cardiology, or general medicine. They can work in hospitals, clinics, research institutions, or start their own practice.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong academic background in biology and chemistry, empathy, and excellent communication skills are essential. Rigorous training and internships are part of the preparation.

Colleges

Top medical colleges include AIIMS, CMC Vellore, and JIPMER in India, and international institutions like Harvard Medical School.

Exams

Key entrance exams include NEET-UG for undergraduate programs and NEET-PG for postgraduate studies in India.

Additional Information

Doctors need to stay updated with medical advancements and continuously enhance their skills. They play a vital role in improving public health and well-being.
