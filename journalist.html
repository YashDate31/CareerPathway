Journalist

Why?

Journalism is a dynamic career that involves reporting news, investigating stories, and shaping public opinion.

Opportunities

Journalists can work in print media, television, or digital platforms. Opportunities also exist in investigative journalism and public relations.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong communication, research, and writing skills are essential. A degree in journalism or mass communication is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMC, ACJ, and Columbia Journalism School.

Exams

Entrance exams like IIMC Entrance Exam and university-specific tests are common for journalism programs.

Additional Information

Journalists need to stay updated with current events and continuously enhance their skills through workshops and certifications.
