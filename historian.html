Historian

Why?

History is a captivating career that involves studying and interpreting past events to understand human civilization and its evolution.

Opportunities

Historians can work in museums, archives, universities, or as authors and researchers. Opportunities also exist in cultural heritage management and public history.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, research abilities, and a passion for history are essential. A degree in history or related fields is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include JNU, Delhi University, and Oxford University.

Exams

Entrance exams like UGC NET (for teaching and research) and university-specific tests are common for history programs.

Additional Information

Historians need to stay updated with new research and continuously enhance their skills through workshops and academic conferences.
