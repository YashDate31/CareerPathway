Sales Manager

Why?

Sales management is a dynamic career that drives business growth by leading sales teams and strategies.

Opportunities

Sales managers can work in retail, corporate firms, or startups. Opportunities also exist in marketing and business development.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong communication skills, leadership, and knowledge of market trends are essential. A degree in business or marketing is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, Harvard Business School, and Wharton.

Exams

Relevant exams include CAT, GMAT, and GRE.

Additional Information

Certifications in sales management and CRM tools can enhance career prospects.
