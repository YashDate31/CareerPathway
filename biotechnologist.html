Biotechnologist

Why?

Biotechnology is a rapidly growing field that combines biology and technology to solve problems in healthcare, agriculture, and environmental science.

Opportunities

Biotechnologists work in industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, food technology, and environmental conservation. They can also engage in research and development.

Strengths/Preparation

A strong foundation in biology, chemistry, and genetics is essential. Practical skills in laboratory techniques and data analysis are also important.

Colleges

Top colleges include the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), and the University of Hyderabad.

Exams

Key entrance exams include JEE Advanced for IITs, GATE for postgraduate studies, and university-specific entrance tests.

Additional Information

Biotechnologists play a crucial role in developing vaccines, improving crop yields, and creating sustainable solutions for global challenges.
