Nutritionist

Why?

Nutrition is a vital career that involves promoting health and wellness through proper dietary practices and nutrition education.

Opportunities

Nutritionists can work in hospitals, wellness centers, or as consultants. Opportunities also exist in sports nutrition and public health.

Strengths/Preparation

Knowledge of food science, communication skills, and a passion for health are essential. A degree in nutrition or dietetics is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include NIN Hyderabad, Lady Irwin College, and University of Sydney.

Exams

Entrance exams like ICAR AIEEA and university-specific tests are common for nutrition programs.

Additional Information

Nutritionists need to stay updated with dietary trends and continuously enhance their skills through certifications and workshops.
