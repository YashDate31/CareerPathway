Architect

Why?

Architecture is a blend of art and science, offering the opportunity to shape the built environment and create spaces that inspire and serve communities.

Opportunities

Architects can work in various fields such as urban planning, interior design, landscape architecture, and sustainable design. They can also specialize in residential, commercial, or industrial projects.

Strengths/Preparation

To excel as an architect, one needs strong visualization skills, creativity, technical knowledge, and proficiency in design software like AutoCAD and Revit. A solid foundation in mathematics and physics is also essential.

Colleges

Top colleges for architecture include the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), School of Planning and Architecture (SPA), and CEPT University.

Exams

Key entrance exams for architecture include NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) and JEE Main (Paper 2).

Additional Information

Architects play a crucial role in sustainable development and green building practices. They often collaborate with engineers, contractors, and clients to bring designs to life.
