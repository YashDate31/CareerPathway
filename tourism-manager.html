Tourism Manager

Why?

Tourism management is a dynamic career that involves planning and promoting travel experiences for individuals and groups.

Opportunities

Tourism managers can work in travel agencies, hotels, or government tourism boards. Opportunities also exist in event planning and marketing.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong organizational skills, creativity, and knowledge of tourism trends are essential. A degree in tourism or hospitality is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IITTM, EHL, and Cornell University.

Exams

Relevant exams include CAT, GMAT, and certifications in tourism management.

Additional Information

Experience in managing travel operations and customer service is crucial for career growth.
