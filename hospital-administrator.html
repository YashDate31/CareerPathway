Hospital Administrator

Why?

Hospital administration is a critical career that involves managing healthcare facilities to ensure efficient delivery of medical services.

Opportunities

Hospital administrators can work in hospitals, clinics, or healthcare consulting firms. Opportunities also exist in public health management and medical tourism.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong leadership, organizational skills, and knowledge of healthcare systems are essential. A degree in hospital management or related fields is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include AIIMS, TISS, and Johns Hopkins University.

Exams

Entrance exams like CAT (for MBA in hospital management) and university-specific tests are common for hospital administration programs.

Additional Information

Hospital administrators need to stay updated with healthcare policies and continuously enhance their skills through training and certifications.
