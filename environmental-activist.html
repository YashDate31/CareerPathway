Environmental Activist

Why?

Environmental activism is a meaningful career for those passionate about protecting the planet and advocating for sustainable practices and policies.

Opportunities

Environmental activists can work with NGOs, government agencies, or international organizations. They can also engage in community outreach, policy advocacy, or research.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong communication skills, passion for environmental issues, and knowledge of sustainability practices are essential. Volunteering and internships can provide valuable experience.

Colleges

Top institutions include TERI University, JNU, and universities offering programs in environmental science or policy.

Exams

Relevant exams include university-specific entrance tests for environmental studies programs.

Additional Information

Environmental activists play a critical role in raising awareness and driving change. Continuous learning and collaboration with like-minded individuals are key to success.
