Environmentalist

Why?

Environmentalists work to protect natural resources and promote sustainable practices, making it a fulfilling career for those passionate about the environment.

Opportunities

Environmentalists can work in research, policy-making, education, or advocacy. They can also collaborate with NGOs, government agencies, or international organizations.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, knowledge of environmental science, and a commitment to sustainability are essential. Fieldwork and internships can provide valuable experience.

Colleges

Top institutions include TERI University, JNU, and universities offering programs in environmental science or policy.

Exams

Relevant exams include university-specific entrance tests for environmental studies programs.

Additional Information

Environmentalists play a critical role in addressing global challenges like climate change and biodiversity loss. Continuous learning and collaboration are key to success.
