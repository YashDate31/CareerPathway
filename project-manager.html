Project Manager

Why?

Project management is a critical career that ensures the successful execution of projects across various industries.

Opportunities

Project managers can work in IT, construction, or healthcare. Opportunities also exist in consulting and operations management.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong organizational skills, leadership, and knowledge of project management tools are essential. A degree in management or engineering is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include IIMs, Harvard Business School, and INSEAD.

Exams

Relevant exams include PMP certification and GMAT.

Additional Information

Experience in managing teams and budgets is crucial for career growth.
