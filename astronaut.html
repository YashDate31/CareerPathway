Astronaut

Why?

Becoming an astronaut is a dream for those passionate about space exploration and pushing the boundaries of human knowledge and capabilities.

Opportunities

Astronauts work with space agencies like NASA, ISRO, and ESA. They participate in space missions, conduct research, and contribute to advancements in science and technology.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong academic background in science, engineering, or medicine is essential. Physical fitness, problem-solving skills, and the ability to work in teams are also critical.

Colleges

Top institutions include the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), and Stanford University.

Exams

Relevant exams include JEE Advanced for IIST, GRE for international programs, and specific astronaut selection tests conducted by space agencies.

Additional Information

Astronauts undergo rigorous training, including simulations, survival training, and technical skill development. They play a vital role in advancing humanity's presence in space.
