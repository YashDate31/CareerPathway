Statistician

Why?

Statistics is a vital career that involves analyzing data to make informed decisions and solve complex problems.

Opportunities

Statisticians can work in research, government agencies, or corporate firms. Opportunities also exist in data science and academia.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, mathematical aptitude, and knowledge of statistical tools are essential. A degree in statistics is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include ISI Kolkata, University of Cambridge, and Harvard University.

Exams

Relevant exams include ISI entrance tests and GRE (for international studies).

Additional Information

Engaging in internships and research projects can enhance career prospects.
