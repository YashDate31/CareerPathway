Forester

Why?

Forestry is a vital career that involves managing and conserving forests, wildlife, and natural resources for ecological balance and sustainability.

Opportunities

Foresters can work in government forest departments, wildlife sanctuaries, NGOs, or as environmental consultants. Opportunities also exist in research and academia.

Strengths/Preparation

Knowledge of ecology, biology, and environmental science is essential. A degree in forestry or related fields and fieldwork experience are beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include FRI Dehradun, TNAU, and Yale School of Forestry & Environmental Studies.

Exams

Entrance exams like IFS (Indian Forest Service) and university-specific tests are common for forestry programs.

Additional Information

Foresters need to stay updated with environmental policies and continuously enhance their skills through training and certifications.
