Physicist

Why?

Physics is a fundamental science that explores the laws of nature, contributing to technological and scientific advancements.

Opportunities

Physicists can work in research, academia, or industries like aerospace and energy. Opportunities also exist in data science and finance.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong analytical skills, mathematical aptitude, and curiosity are essential. A degree in physics or related fields is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include MIT, IISc Bangalore, and University of Cambridge.

Exams

Relevant exams include JEST, GATE, and GRE (for international studies).

Additional Information

Engaging in research projects and internships can provide valuable experience.
