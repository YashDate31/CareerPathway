CareerPathway

Teacher

Why?

Teaching is a noble career that shapes the future by imparting knowledge and values to students.

Opportunities

Teachers can work in schools, colleges, or as private tutors. Opportunities also exist in educational consulting and curriculum design.

Strengths/Preparation

Strong communication skills, patience, and subject expertise are essential. A degree in education or the subject of specialization is required.

Colleges

Top institutions include Delhi University, Harvard Graduate School of Education, and NCERT.

Exams

Relevant exams include CTET, TET, and NET (for higher education).

Additional Information

Continuous professional development and certifications in teaching methodologies can enhance career prospects.
