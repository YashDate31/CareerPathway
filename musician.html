Musician

Why?

Music is a creative career that allows individuals to express emotions and connect with audiences through melodies and rhythms.

Opportunities

Musicians can work as performers, composers, or music producers. Opportunities also exist in teaching and sound engineering.

Strengths/Preparation

Creativity, dedication, and proficiency in musical instruments or vocals are essential. Formal training in music is beneficial.

Colleges

Top institutions include Berklee College of Music, Juilliard School, and KM Music Conservatory.

Exams

No specific exams, but auditions and certifications in music are valuable.

Additional Information

Musicians need to stay updated with industry trends and continuously enhance their skills through practice and collaborations.
